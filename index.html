Monte Gordo

  • Formerly a fishermen's town, Monte Gordo is located in southeastern corner of Algarve, Portugal.
  • Monte Gordo's sunny weather, long white sand beaches, sand dunes and trails in the surrounding pine woods are a joy to be experienced!
  • The adjacent natural reserve - Reserva Natural do Sapal de Castro Marim e Vila Real de Santo Antonio - attracts nature lovers.
  • Monte Gordo's closeness to nearby touristic attractions such as Cacela Velha and Tavira is also a plus.
